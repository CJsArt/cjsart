What is a DIY Tool Closet?

Have you ever been tired of misplacing your tools? Do you live in an apartment or condo and don't have a garage to store your tools? Have you tried storing your tools in your truck just for your truck to get broken into and all your tools stolen? Well, I relate to 2 of those questions. Unfortunately, for my neighbor, he relates to the last question. Yes, he had $3,000 worth of tools stolen out of his truck! You think he would have learned and built himself a tool closet but no, he just replaced his tools and put them back in the same truck and yes got them stolen again!

Finished Tool Closet

This is my finished tool closet. I built shelves on the out side of it to house my baby gecko. I also put my service dogs bowls on it.

Up-Cycling and Repurposing

I got so tired of never being able to find my tools. So I gathered up a bunch of scrap wood and screws that I could find. I mostly harvested my screws from tossed out electronics. I harvested a lot of my wood from the craigslist free section. Yes it was a lot of hard work. I would dismantle large things people built and now wanted removed from their property. I had to dismantle them to small enough parts to load in my car or on the roof of my car. I learned the hard way that if anyone tells you that you don't need to bring your tools and that they have some to still bring your own. I drove 2 hours and didn't bring my drill because the guy said he had all the tools to dismantle it. What tools did he have you ask? Well, just 1, an adjustable crescent wrench. That was one of the hardest harvesting of repurposed parts I ever did because it was in the northern states or America and dead winter. My fingers were so cold I couldn't grip the wrench.
